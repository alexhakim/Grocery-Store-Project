##### Shopper
As a shopper I want to easily be able to sync the scanner to my personal account on my mobile phone app					
As a shopper I want to be able to scan items that I place in my cart using the scanner					
As a shopper I want to be able to see the list of items that I have scanned on my phone as I am shopping					
As a shopper I want to be able to remove some of the items that I have scanned from my cart using my phone					
As a shopper I wanna be able to login to my account					
As a shopper I want to be able to log out of my account					
As a shopper I want to be able to see a page with nutrition facts driven from my past 10 shopping events					
As a shopper I want to be able to see a graph of my spending history within the past 10 events					
As a shopper I want to have a check out button that produces a bar code that I can use to check out with.					
							
##### Application					
As the application I need to store customers data categorized based on their customer number					
As the application I need to recieve incoming bar codes from the scanner					
As the application I need to find and fetch from database the items that their barcodes were passed to me 					
As the application I need to regularly check my connection with the Scanner device
